Game-Clock

An arcade-cabinet game clock driven by an Arduino Nano — a MAX7219 dot-matrix display, joystick, and button for control, housed in a 3D-printed arcade-machine enclosure.

  • Arduino Nano
  • C/C++
  • MAX7219 Matrix
  • 3D Printing
PROJ.02 In progress

SEEDR — Reforestation Drone

An agriculture quadcopter that drops grass seeds through a servo-actuated, 3D-printed dispensing mechanism (modeled after a flour dispenser). Remote-controlled today, with autonomous flight next on the roadmap.

  • Pixhawk
  • Servo Actuation
  • Fusion 360
  • Carbon Fiber
PROJ.03 In progress

Hand-Tracking Robotic Arm

A robotic arm that mirrors hand movement in real time using MediaPipe vision tracking, with voice-command control in the works. Next step: 3D-printing the full arm.

  • Python
  • MediaPipe
  • Computer Vision
  • Servos
PROJ.04 Planned

Energy Grid Simulator

A power-grid simulator that toggles between wall-socket power, solar, and a custom piezoelectric generator I'll be building — visualizing how energy sourcing shifts across the grid.
